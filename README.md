Overview

This project delivers a Behaviour Analysis Dashboard built using Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Power BI to analyze customer purchasing patterns, revenue drivers, and profitability trends. The objective is to convert raw transactional data into decision-grade insights for business stakeholders.

Key Business Metrics

The dashboard surfaces high-level KPIs to provide immediate situational awareness:

Total Revenue: 3.52M
Total Orders: 1,000
Total Profit: 704K
Average Delivery Time: 5.53 days
Average Revenue per Order: $3.5K

These metrics indicate a healthy profit margin (~20%) and relatively stable operational efficiency.



Analytical Insights

1. Revenue & Sales Trends
Monthly sales show high volatility, with peaks crossing ~$500K and troughs near ~$100K.
Strong spikes indicate seasonal or campaign-driven demand, especially around specific months.
Revenue concentration suggests dependence on few high-performing periods.

2. Profitability Analysis
Profit follows revenue trends but with inconsistent margins, implying cost fluctuations.
Highest profits observed in later months (~$140K+), suggesting improved efficiency or pricing strategy.

3. Customer Occasion Behavior
Sales distribution across occasions reveals:
Anniversary & Raksha Bandhan contribute significantly
“All Occasions” category still holds ~17% share
Indicates strong event-driven purchasing behavior, useful for targeted marketing.

4. Geographic Performance
Top-performing cities (e.g., Imphal, Haridwar, Dhanbad) dominate order volumes.
Revenue concentration in specific regions suggests localized demand clusters.
Opportunity exists to expand underperforming regions.

5. Product Performance
Revenue is heavily skewed toward top 3–5 products.
Long-tail products contribute minimally (~12% lowest).
Indicates a Pareto distribution (80/20 effect) in product sales.



Interactive visualizations:
KPI Cards
Line Charts (Sales Trends)
Bar Charts (Profit Analysis)
Treemaps (City-level Orders)
Donut Charts (Occasion-based Sales)
Slicers for dynamic filtering (Category, Month, Occasion)


Business Impact
Enables data-driven decision making by consolidating key metrics into a single view
Identifies high-value customers, products, and regions
Supports targeted marketing campaigns based on occasion-driven demand
Improves inventory and supply chain planning using trend insights
Highlights profit leakage areas for cost optimization


Strategic Recommendations
Capitalize on seasonal peaks with pre-planned campaigns
Diversify product portfolio to reduce dependency on top sellers
Optimize logistics to reduce delivery time further
Expand in low-performing cities using localized strategies
Implement customer segmentation models for deeper behavioral insights

Conclusion

This dashboard transitions raw data into actionable intelligence, offering both macro-level visibility and micro-level diagnostics. It demonstrates a strong foundation in data analytics, visualization, and business interpretation, aligning technical execution with strategic business value.
