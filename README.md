# Behaviour Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents a **Behaviour Analysis Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Excel** and **Microsoft Power BI**. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights that help understand customer behavior, sales performance, and profitability trends.

---

## Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** 3.52M  
- **Total Orders:** 1,000  
- **Total Profit:** 704K  
- **Average Delivery Time:** 5.53 days  
- **Average Revenue per Order:** $3.5K  

---

## Dashboard Insights

### 1. Sales Performance
- Monthly sales show significant fluctuations.
- Peak sales exceed **$500K**, indicating strong seasonal or campaign-driven demand.
- Lower sales months highlight potential gaps in engagement or marketing.

### 2. Profit Analysis
- Profit trends generally align with revenue.
- Higher profits observed in later months suggest improved efficiency or pricing.
- Margin inconsistencies indicate possible cost variations.

### 3. Occasion-Based Sales
- Major contribution from:
  - Anniversary
  - Raksha Bandhan
- “All Occasions” category contributes ~17% of sales.
- Strong evidence of **event-driven purchasing behavior**.

### 4. Geographic Insights
- Top cities include:
  - Imphal
  - Haridwar
  - Dhanbad
- Sales are concentrated in specific regions.
- Opportunity to expand into underperforming cities.

### 5. Product Performance
- Revenue is dominated by top-performing products.
- Lower-tier products contribute minimal revenue (~12%).
- Indicates **Pareto distribution (80/20 rule)**.
